How it works

Stage 1

First Pass

Same question sent to 9 seats — 3 frontier models × 3 personas (first-principles, contrarian, executor). Each answers independently.

Stage 2

Peer Review

Every seat reviews anonymized answers from all other seats. Scored on accuracy, completeness, reasoning, usefulness, clarity.

Stage 3

Synthesis

Chairman model reads everything, ranks by Borda count, identifies blind spots and disagreements, writes the final answer.
